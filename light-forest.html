Interactive Light Forest





Concept

For the theme 'The Winter Playground' a model of a playful winter forest was created. Children are meant to interact with the trees, and so the lights start to play.



Goal

In the course Physical Interaction during my master we were asked to create a concept for play in the cold, dark and wet nordic winters. Encourage movement and social play.


Outcome

By visual feedback of the lights together with tactile feedback through touching the pearl curtains and the fiber light users enjoyed the expeience of the forest. Children were more rough than adults which resulted in effective feedback.
People were positively surprised when they managed to trigger the 'rainbow effect'.
