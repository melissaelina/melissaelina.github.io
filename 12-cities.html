12 Cities for the City Life



Summary

In 2018, I, along with Linnéa Granlund and Sofia Blomgren embarked on the adventure of planning and leading the student reception at the chapter for Media Technology at KTH.

The reception took place four weeks in August-September, but the work as manager began by Christmas the year before.


Goal

To welcome the new students at the media technology programme with a four week long reception. Create a space where they feel safe and welcome. And introduce them to the life as a student at KTH.

Also create a safe and productive space for the forty team members all working to create the reception.
